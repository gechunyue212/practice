绿色能源云矿机 Ownership of Energy

用户拥有的ENB作为权益凭证可以认购用户专属的部分或多台云矿机。云矿机对应的能量每天会从矿池中获得相应的收益。所有的产出基于现实世界里分布式风力发电机、分布式光伏发电机对绿色能源的采集。 The ENB owned by the user as an equity voucher can subscribe to the user's own part or multiple cloud mining machines. The corresponding energy of the cloud mining machine will get corresponding income from the mine pool every day. All outputs are based on the acquisition of green energy by distributed wind generators and distributed photovoltaic generators in the real world.

FIP分布式智慧型矿场 Dual-token Incentives

FIP基于新一代绿色能源矿机建设而成，电力成本低廉。 建设FIP的同时协助改善当地能源结构，助力当地能源供需改革。 FIP从供应到产出所有环节全部使用绿色能源，实体矿机的运行数据严格上链。 FIP is based on a new generation of green energy mining machines with low power costs. While building the FIP, it will help improve the local energy structure and help the local energy supply and demand reform. FIP uses green energy in all aspects from supply to output, and the operational data of the physical mining machine will strictly be uploaded on the chain.
